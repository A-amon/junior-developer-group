Q: What tools and technologies we use? Answer:

For a large majority of our projects we use HTML/CSS and vanilla JavaScript. However, we're slowly moving on to building projects using JS frameworks such as Vue.

As for tools, we use Figma for our designs, Jira for ticket management (we work in sprints), Discord as a main communication platform and Gather for our Friday Focus Sessions.

Q: Is there a skill level required to join the team? Answer:

The team is purposely created for beginners and junior developers alike. We have no skill or experience requirements and hope you'll be able to gain some while participating on our projects instead.

Q: Are there team meetings? Answer:

Since the group was created we've always met once a week. Always on Fridays at 7 PM UK Time. Sometimes we will schedule extra workshops/events which we also notify all of our members about before picking date/time for each one.

Q: Do I need to be online and participating every day? Answer:

In short, no. We run this group in a very casual way and we respect everyone has jobs and other responsibilities outside of it. Participation is completely optional.
