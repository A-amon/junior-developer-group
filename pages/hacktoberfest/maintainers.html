[INFO] Who are HacktoberFest Maintainers?

Who are Maintainers?

Maintainers are people who are responsible for reviewing, accepting/rejecting, and helping around created Pull Requests on our HacktoberFest repository.

What skills do I need to be a Maintainer?
  1. You'll need to be comfortable using Git/GitHub features

    (seeing changes made on PR you're reviewing, accepting the PR, rejecting, approving, commenting on, tagging onto PRs, merging, solving conflicts etc)

  2. You'll need to be comfortable reading other people's code

    (HTML/CSS/vanilla JS)

  3. You'll need to be able to evaluate other people's code based on their current skill level

    (if they copied it from someone else, if their PR messed something else up on the page, if third party library/framework/plugin has been used, etc)

  4. You'll need to be able to give appropriate feedback for the PRs submitted

    (if and when needed - I expect this to be rare)

  5. You'll need to be comfortable keeping 'the house in order'
How long do I need to be a Maintainer for?

We will be split into Maintainers and Helpers for the whole of October - until the challenge has passed. After HacktoberFest we'll be restarting our usual group activities and Maintainers will get a chance to become Team Leads, depending on their HacktoberFest engagements and overall group activity.

[INFO] Who are HacktoberFest Helpers ?

Who are Helpers?

HacktoberFest Helpers are the glue of this event. They keep the house in order, answer people's questions, and point them in the right direction, they get involved in planning and organizing but aren't required to review, approve or reject any PRs at all.

What skills do I need to be a Helper?
  1. You will need to be comfortable answering questions around our community, relevant links, information on joining us for further collaboration, and similar questions.
  2. You will need to be keeping an eye on the number of open PRs we have and alert Maintainers if it's becoming stale and no one picked any up.
  3. You will be included in planning/organising and building up the skeleton of our group's repository and need to be comfortable sharing your ideas and taking ownership of actions.
How long do I need to be a Helper for?

We will be split into Maintainers and Helpers for the whole of October - until the challenge has passed. After HacktoberFest we'll be restarting our usual group activities and Helpers will get a chance to become Community Ambassadors depending on their HacktoberFest engagements and overall group activity.
